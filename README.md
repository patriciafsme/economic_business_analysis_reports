# Economic & Digital Transformation Analysis Reports

## Project Overview
This repository contains a collection of strategic analysis reports focusing on socioeconomic indicators and the digital transformation of the business sector. These studies demonstrate my ability to handle large public datasets, perform comparative analysis, and translate raw data into actionable business and economic insights.

## Included Case Studies

### 1. Socioeconomic Analysis: Greece vs. EU-27
* **Objective:** Comparative study of Greece's economic recovery and performance relative to the European Union (EU-27) across different expansionary and recessionary cycles.
* **Data Source:** Eurostat.
* **Key Metrics:** GDP per capita, labor productivity, employment rates, and convergence indices.
* **Insights:** Analysis of the productivity gap and the structural factors limiting Greece's economic growth compared to its European peers.

### 2. Digital Economy: Big Data Adoption in Spanish Companies
* **Objective:** Analysis of Big Data usage and TIC (Information and Communication Technologies) equipment across various business sectors in Spain.
* **Data Source:** INE (Spanish National Statistics Institute).
* **Focus Areas:** Analysis by company size (SME vs. Large Enterprise), sector-specific adoption rates, and the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on digital acceleration.
* **Insights:** Identification of barriers to entry for Big Data analytics in smaller firms and the digital leadership of the services sector.

## Methodology & Tools
* **Data Sourcing:** Advanced extraction from official statistical databases (Eurostat/INE).
* **Analysis:** Quantitative analysis of growth rates, contribution of components to GDP, and sectoral benchmarking.
* **Visualization & Reporting:** Data visualization through Excel and professional reporting highlighting key takeaways for stakeholders.
